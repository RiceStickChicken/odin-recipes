Breakfast Burrito

Description

A fast DIY breakfast for the road that's made in minutes with scrambled egg, salsa, and cheese wrapped up in a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice reduced-fat American cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Crack the eggs, add the salsa, and stir.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute, stir, and cook until firm.
  4. Place cheese in center of tortilla and top with egg mixture.
  5. Wrap it up like a burrito.
